Saegis codepulse 2023 - Hackathon

Hackathon Details

Saegis codepulse 2023 Hackathon is a prestigious competition that aims to nurture the innovative spirit and software development skills of undergraduate students from state and private universities / institutes in Sri Lanka. This Hackathon comprises two rounds, with the top-performing teams advancing to the final round and the opportunity to win attractive cash prizes.

Event Overview

Team Size A maximum of four members in a team
Eligibility All team members must be current undergraduates of a recognized state university or higher education institute in Sri Lanka. Team members should be from the same university or institute, and a team must consist of exactly four members.
Technologies Participants have the flexibility to choose their preferred technologies for their project.

Rules and Regulations

  • Team members who have registered and participated in the initial round will be eligible for the final round.
  • Once a team is registered, members cannot be replaced with new members.

Number of Rounds

The Hackathon consists of two rounds.

Round 1: Initial Round (Online)

Date To be Announced
Problem Definition Participating teams will create and submit a video presenting a software system. Detailed guidelines will be provided after the application closing date.
Selection Process A panel of judges will evaluate submissions to select the top 10 teams.
Evaluation Criteria Evaluation criteria will be made available on the official website after the application closing date.

Round 2: Final Round (On-Campus)

Date To be Announced
Problem Definition Shortlisted teams will engage in an intense 10-12 hour Hackathon, where they will work on solving a real-world software challenge.
Selection Process Judges will evaluate the teams' solutions to select the top 3 teams for awards.
Evaluation Criteria Detailed evaluation criteria will be provided during the competition.
